Terms & Definitions

Understand the reports generated for your banner and email campaigns

This section helps clients better understand the report fields used in IIR Direct banner and email campaign reporting. Banner reports use only a small set of applicable fields, while email reports include broader delivery, engagement, and click activity definitions.

Banner reports are not sold on impressions or clicks, so many export fields may be left empty. The main applicable banner definitions are:
  • Impr — Impression (# of times your banner is viewed)
  • Clicks — Clicks on your banner
  • Ctr — Click Through Ratio (# of Clicks / # of Impressions)

Email reports may include delivery, open, read, unsubscribe, and click activity. Common fields include:
  • Subject — The subject of your email
  • Sent On — The date emails started sending
  • Delivered — Successfully delivered emails
  • Unsubscribed / Unsubscribed %
  • Opened / Opened %
  • Read / Read %
  • Clicks / Clicks %
  • Total / Repeat / New — Click activity breakdown
  • Date Viewed — Exact time opened
  • Date Clicked — Exact time clicked

If you still have questions about an email or banner report, IIR Direct directs clients to contact support for help. ncole@iirdirect.com
